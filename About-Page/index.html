About Us

Welcome! Our journey began in 2019 as the Casual Arma Bois, a small group of friends exploring Antistasi together. Since then, we've grown into a thriving community dedicated to enhancing the Antistasi experience.

Over time, we've collaborated with like-minded individuals - We've pooled our talents to create Antistasi Ultimate, a mod that expands and improves upon the Antistasi Plus mod.

Join us in our mission to redefine fun and shape the future of Antistasi Ultimate. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, there's a place for you in our community.

Join the Antistasi Ultimate Community—Where Every Player Matters!
